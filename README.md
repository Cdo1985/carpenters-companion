# Carpenter's Companion (AUS v1.0)
A specialized toolkit for Australian carpenters, plasterers, and commercial wall/ceiling installers. Designed for use on-site in Melbourne and across Victoria.

## üöÄ Key Features

### 1. Compliance & Evidence Log (NEW)
Document your work for site managers and inspectors with ease.
- **Multi-Image Upload:** Attach up to 5 photos per entry to show evidence of satisfactory work (e.g., fire pillows, seismic joints).
- **Area & Date Tracking:** Log the specific location (e.g., "Level 2 North Bulkhead") and the exact date of completion.
- **Secure Persistence:** All evidence is saved locally to your device, ensuring data is kept even if you refresh or switch tabs.

### 2. Digital Handbook
A comprehensive technical reference library at your fingertips.
- **Caulking Guide:** Best application practices for Fire Mastic (FRL), Acoustic Sealant, Sanitary Silicone, and Polyurethane joints.
- **Construction Math:** Essential hacks like the **3-4-5 Square Rule**, Top Hat Do's & Don'ts, and quick area formulas.
- **Steel Framing (Rondo/Studco):** Detailed specs for over 30 variants, including 150mm C-Studs and Slotted Deflection Tracks.
- **Commercial Guides:** Direct access to Seismic Design (AS 1170.4) and Fire-Rated (FRL) system selectors.
- **Timber Standards:** AS 1684 quick reference for stud spacing, noggins, and wind classifications (N1-N4).

### 3. Site Trade Defect Tracker
Monitor issues that impact your workflow or structural integrity.
- **Smart State Management:** Remembers your inputs and logged issues automatically using LocalStorage.
- **Templates:** Fast-log common site issues like missing noggins or incorrect board types.
- **Standards-Based:** Tips integrated from the **VBA Guide to Standards & Tolerances 2015**.

### 4. Wall & Ceiling Estimator
Generate quick labor and material estimates.
- **Flexible Modes:** Switch between Wall and Ceiling estimators.
- **Materials:** Calculate framing (Timber/Steel), Insulation, Sheeting, and Troweling requirements.
- **Labor Quote:** Instant dollar estimates based on customizable hourly rates.
